Player Position vs. Transfer Fees and Frequency Scatter Plot

  • Each circle's position in the plot indicates the actual on-field position.
  • For each position, darker colors indicate higher transfer frequency and a larger radius indicates higher average transfer fees.
  • It can be observed that forwards have higher average transfer fees and transfer frequency, indicating their higher popularity.
  • Comparatively, left-sided players have higher average transfer fees and transfer frequency than their right-sided counterparts, highlighting the importance of the left side in football.
  • Among defenders, goalkeepers have a higher average transfer fee.
