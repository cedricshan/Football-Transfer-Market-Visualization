Transfer Fee vs. Age Box Plot

  • Each point represents a transfer record, and the orange line indicates the average transfer fee changing with age.
  • It can be observed that the average transfer fee peaks around the age of 24 for players (may vary across different leagues), implying that clubs have the highest expectations based on a player's development potential and ability at this age.
