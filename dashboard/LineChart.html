Total Transfer Fee vs. Year for Different Leagues Line Chart

  • Overall, the total transfer fees in major European leagues have increased year by year with economic development and inflation.
  • The acquisition of Chelsea by Roman Abramovich in 2003 triggered a surge in the English Premier League (EPL), surpassing Italian Serie A in total transfer fees.
  • In recent years, the EPL consistently maintains the highest total transfer fees, especially after the pandemic, showing significant dominance.
  • During the pandemic, the transfer market experienced a collective, sharp decline.
